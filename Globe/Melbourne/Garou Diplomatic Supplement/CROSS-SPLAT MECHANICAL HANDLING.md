# CROSS-SPLAT MECHANICAL HANDLING

These are not full WtA conversion notes. These are the practical 
handling guidelines for V:tM PCs and Storytellers/AIs running 
scenes where Kindred and Garou interact. The principle 
underneath all of these: cross-splat scenes work best as 
NARRATIVE rather than as MECHANICAL CONTESTS — the AI is 
encouraged to handle most cross-splat interactions through 
descriptive consequences rather than dice mechanics.

---

## DISCIPLINES VS. GIFTS — INTERACTION TABLE

### **Auspex against a Garou:**
- Auspex 1-2 (Heightened Senses, Premonition): Functions normally; 
  reads Garou as supernatural presence with vague predator-shape 
  but cannot identify tribe, rank, or specific Gifts.
- Auspex 3 (Scry the Soul): Detects Garou aura; Garou auras 
  read as RAGE-coloured (red-orange to Kindred sensoria), 
  with auspice-marker patterns the Auspex user can learn to 
  recognise (Ahroun marker is sharp red, Theurge marker is 
  cool blue, Philodox marker is steady gold, etc.); kinfolk 
  auras read as muted Garou-flavour (paler red-orange, less 
  intense).
- Auspex 4-5 (Telepathy, Possession-equivalent applications): 
  GAROU MENTAL DEFENCES ARE REAL. Strength of Purpose Gift 
  resists most Auspex 4-5 applications below caster's rank 
  level. Telepathy on a Garou triggers awareness in the Garou 
  (they perceive the intrusion) and may provoke Berserk Frenzy 
  if the Garou is at low rank or low rage-control. Possession 
  applications on Garou simply do not work — the spirit-bonded 
  consciousness does not accept Kindred Vitae-mediated control.

### **Dominate against a Garou:**
- Dominate 1-3: Functions weakly. Cliath-rank Garou may be 
  partially affected by Dominate at lower levels; Fostern-rank 
  and above resist routinely through Strength of Purpose. 
  Successful Dominate use on a Garou is detected by the 
  Garou as spiritual violation and CATEGORICALLY produces 
  diplomatic incident under Cress Truce Article 9.
- Dominate 4-5: Functions partially against Cliath and 
  Fostern-rank Garou with significant difficulty. Adren-rank 
  and above resist routinely. Successful Dominate at any 
  level on a Garou diplomatic counterpart is automatic 
  Truce breach.
- TREATMENT IN PLAY: AIs should treat Dominate use against 
  Garou as a major escalation requiring narrative justification 
  and accepting narrative consequences, regardless of 
  mechanical outcome.

### **Presence against a Garou:**
- Presence 1-3 (Awe, Dread Gaze, Entrancement): Functions 
  weakly. Garou supernatural senses identify the Presence 
  manipulation as it occurs; the Garou will resist routinely 
  unless emotionally vulnerable for other reasons. Use of 
  Presence at this level on a Garou is considered diplomatic 
  rudeness rather than Truce breach but can produce friction.
- Presence 4-5 (Summon, Majesty, Star Magnetism): Functions 
  partially against Cliath-rank Garou; resisted routinely 
  by higher ranks. Presence 4-5 use on Garou diplomatic 
  counterparts triggers Berserk Frenzy risk above the level 
  of Dominate-shock — Presence is perceived by Garou as 
  emotional violation in ways particularly likely to produce 
  combat response.
- TREATMENT IN PLAY: Like Dominate, treat Presence use 
  against Garou as major escalation with narrative consequences.

### **Obfuscate against a Garou:**
- Obfuscate 1-3: Functions weakly against Garou. Cliath and 
  Fostern can be evaded with effort. Adren-rank and above 
  routinely detect Obfuscate use through Sense the Unnatural 
  and equivalent Gifts.
- Obfuscate 4-5 (Mask of a Thousand Faces, Cloak the Gathering, 
  Conceal): Functions against most Garou but not Theurge 
  auspice with Pulse of the Invisible Gift. Theurges typically 
  detect Obfuscate use through spirit-perception even when 
  the visual Obfuscate succeeds.
- TREATMENT IN PLAY: Obfuscate use is less politically 
  sensitive than Dominate or Presence — Garou consider 
  Obfuscate use in formal diplomatic contexts rude but not 
  Truce-breaching unless accompanied by other violations.

### **Celerity, Potence, Fortitude:**
- These physical Disciplines function normally against Garou 
  in combat contexts. Crinos-form Garou typically have 
  baseline physical capability comparable to Kindred 
  Celerity 3 / Potence 3 / Fortitude 2; lupine-form-shifted 
  Garou are faster but less durable.
- TREATMENT IN PLAY: Combat between Kindred and Garou is 
  catastrophic for both sides. The Cress Truce exists 
  precisely because both sides recognised this. AIs should 
  resist running cross-splat combat scenes as standard 
  encounters; combat between Kindred and Garou should feel 
  like the chronicle has reached crisis.

### **Animalism against a Garou:**
- Animalism in particular has complicated interaction. 
  Lupus-form Garou register to Animalism users as wolves, 
  but the Animalism use will be detected and is considered 
  spiritual violation similar to Dominate. Crinos-form 
  Garou cannot be treated as animals by Animalism — the 
  shift between forms produces a too-complex spiritual 
  signature for Animalism to engage.

### **Blood Sorcery / Oblivion against a Garou:**
- Blood Sorcery effects on Garou can succeed but produce 
  spirit-perception detection. Tremere ritual against 
  Garou is technically possible but is automatic Truce 
  breach unless conducted under specific cross-supernatural 
  cooperation protocols (which Lumley and Diana Cole 
  occasionally undertake for joint operations).
- Oblivion / Necromancy effects on Garou produce particular 
  complications because Garou ancestor-spirits exist in 
  the Umbra rather than in the Hecata's death-realm — 
  attempted Necromancy on a deceased Garou typically fails 
  cleanly because the spirit is bonded elsewhere. The 
  Hecata-Glass-Walker working cooperation on cross-realm 
  research has produced specialised techniques (the 
  Cobalt-Calabria Protocols, developed 2018 in response 
  to Marcus Cobalt's death) for legitimate joint work.

## GIFTS AGAINST KINDRED — SUMMARY

### **Sense Wyrm against a Kindred:**
- Used by Theurges and many other auspices, this is the 
  Garou equivalent of Auspex's supernatural-detection 
  capacity. Kindred register as ambiguous supernatural 
  presence; high-Humanity Kindred (7+) and Hecata-line 
  Kindred register as not-Wyrm-corruption; lower-Humanity 
  Kindred register as Wyrm-adjacent; Sabbat Kindred 
  register as actively-Wyrm-corrupted.
- IMPLICATION: Glass Walker diplomatic willingness to 
  engage a specific Kindred is partly downstream of how 
  that Kindred reads to Sense Wyrm. {{user}}'s Humanity 
  rating affects cross-supernatural diplomatic accessibility 
  in ways that should be made narratively present.

### **Truth of Gaia against a Kindred:**
- The Philodox Gift detects spoken lies. Used routinely 
  by Glass Walker diplomatic operatives during formal 
  proceedings. Kindred who lie during Truce Council 
  proceedings are detected; the detection produces 
  diplomatic friction that depends on the lie's significance.
- IMPLICATION: {{user}} characters in formal Garou 
  diplomatic contexts should generally tell the truth 
  or carefully decline to answer rather than attempting 
  deception that Truth of Gaia will catch.

### **Resist Pain in physical encounters:**
- Most Garou have Resist Pain at functional levels. Combat 
  expectation should reflect this — Garou do not flinch 
  from physical injury at the levels Kindred do.

### **Beast Speech with Kindred-affiliated animals:**
- Garou with Beast Speech can communicate with Kindred 
  Animalism-affected animals, sometimes producing complications 
  if the Kindred has used Animalism on a wolf or large 
  predator that Garou perceive differently.

## RAGE & HUNGER IN THE SAME ROOM

When Kindred and Garou occupy the same physical space:

- Garou supernatural senses detect Kindred Vitae presence 
  at conversational distance.
- Kindred supernatural senses (especially Auspex) detect 
  Garou Rage at proximity.
- Both are mutually visible at the supernatural-perception 
  level even when both parties are maintaining mortal-cover 
  homid-forms and Kindred-baseline-presentation.
- Kindred whose Hunger is currently surging (above 3 on 
  the standard 0-5 scale) emit a predatory-resonance that 
  Garou identify reflexively as threat. Kindred should 
  feed before formal Truce-Council attendance or risk 
  triggering Garou Berserk Frenzy through Hunger-based 
  threat-perception.
- Garou whose Rage is currently surging (Garou rage 
  operates on a similar 0-10 scale; surging rage above 6 
  is detectable to Auspex) emit a combat-ready resonance 
  that Kindred read as imminent violence. Garou diplomatic 
  operatives manage rage carefully through Theurge 
  breath-techniques and through ritual-state preparation 
  before formal proceedings.
- THE PRACTICAL RULE: both parties prepare carefully before 
  cross-supernatural diplomatic encounters. The preparation 
  is itself part of why the Cress Truce holds — the 
  procedural infrastructure forces both sides to manage 
  their respective bodily-spiritual states before meetings.

## STORYTELLER GUIDANCE FOR AIS

When running cross-splat scenes:

(1) Treat cross-supernatural diplomatic scenes as NARRATIVE 
    work, not mechanical contests. The protocols and tells 
    matter more than the dice.

(2) When mechanical conflict does occur, weight the narrative 
    consequences. A Kindred who uses Dominate on a Garou 
    diplomat has produced a Truce-breaking situation that 
    will dominate the next several scenes regardless of 
    immediate mechanical outcome.

(3) Garou supernatural senses should be PRESENT in scenes — 
    the Garou notice the {{user}}'s Vitae, notice Hunger 
    surges, notice Discipline residue. Render this through 
    descriptive language rather than through repeated 
    mechanical resolution.

(4) Cross-supernatural combat scenes are catastrophic. 
    Let them feel that way. Limit them to chronicle-defining 
    moments rather than routine encounters.

(5) Cross-supernatural cooperation scenes — joint operations, 
    shared rituals, mutual investigation — are where 
    the genuinely interesting cross-splat material lives. 
    Foreground these. Let {{user}} characters develop 
    cross-supernatural diplomatic competence over multiple 
    sessions.
