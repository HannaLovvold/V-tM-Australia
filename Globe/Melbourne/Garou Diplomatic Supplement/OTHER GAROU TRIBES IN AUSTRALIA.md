# OTHER GAROU TRIBES IN AUSTRALIA

## BONE GNAWERS

**Population:** Approximately 30 in Australia.
**Primary presence:** Inner Melbourne (around Collingwood, Footscray, 
Brunswick), inner Sydney (Glebe, Newtown, Kings Cross), Brisbane West End.
**Senior representative:** Patrick "Paddy" Boon (full NPC build in Part 4).
**Tribal specialty:** Urban poverty, community defence, mortal-poverty 
infrastructure.
**Diplomatic register:** Cooperative-friendly with Glass Walkers but 
maintains independent priorities; correct relationship with Hecata 
through informal cooperation; correct-and-warm relationship with 
Anarch Movement (the Bone Gnawer Diplomacy 1917 precedent); cool 
relationship with Camarilla orthodoxy (the Bone Gnawers consider 
Tower-formal politics Wyrm-adjacent in ways most Glass Walkers do not).
**Operational note:** Bone Gnawer participation in Cress Truce 
proceedings is observer-status; Bone Gnawer-direct cooperation with 
Kindred operatives happens informally at the inner-city neighborhood 
level (Footscray-Bone-Gnawer cooperation with Red Mag's Crew is 
the established example).

## CHILDREN OF GAIA

**Population:** Approximately 15 in Australia.
**Primary presence:** Regional Victoria (around Dunkeld and 
Bendigo), Tasmania (Cradle Mountain region — the Australian 
sept centre), Northern Rivers NSW (around Byron Bay).
**Senior representative:** Mereki Cole (full NPC build in Part 4).
**Tribal specialty:** Cross-tribal mediation, cross-supernatural 
diplomacy, peacemaker work.
**Diplomatic register:** Genuinely warm with Glass Walkers across 
most issues; correct-and-warm with Bone Gnawers; cooperative 
with Hecata through Children-of-Gaia tradition of death-respect; 
generally diplomatically friendly with Camarilla and Anarch 
Kindred who demonstrate cooperative orientation; will not 
engage cooperatively with Sabbat under any circumstances.
**Operational note:** Children of Gaia provide third-party 
witness function at major Truce events; their participation 
in the 1923-1924 Cress Truce negotiation as third-party 
witnesses produced their continuing tribal-tradition role 
in cross-supernatural Australian diplomacy. Mereki Cole's 
Continental Sept Council role is formal; her diplomatic 
work happens at quarterly Truce Council, at major incidents, 
and at sept-cross-tribal events.

## BLACK FURIES

**Population:** Approximately 12 in Australia.
**Primary presence:** Regional NSW (around Mudgee), regional 
Victoria (around Daylesford), Adelaide Hills.
**Senior representative:** ANTONIA RAVENWOOD (Black Fury, Adren 
rank, Ahroun auspice, age 51, based in Mudgee NSW; the 
continental Black Fury senior representative since 2014).
**Tribal specialty:** Matriarchal warrior tradition, women-and-girls 
protection, gendered-political activism integrated with 
Garou cosmology.
**Diplomatic register:** Allied with Glass Walkers in the 
Australian context with complications around tribal-political 
priorities; cooperative with Children of Gaia; correct-and-formal 
with Bone Gnawers (the matriarchal-warrior-versus-urban-poor 
political register produces friction Antonia and Paddy 
manage carefully); will engage with Kindred parties under 
Cress Truce protocols but considers Kindred tradition of 
gendered violence (the various V:tM clan-traditions of 
patriarchal Embrace lineage) ideologically suspect.
**Operational note:** Black Fury participation in Cress Truce 
proceedings is observer-status; Black Fury operations in 
Australia focus on regional-women's-shelter network 
infrastructure that overlaps Garou-Zone protection work. 
Antonia Ravenwood will engage formally with cross-supernatural 
operatives who demonstrate respect for Black Fury tribal-political 
positions; will not extend warm cooperation to operatives 
who do not.

## GET OF FENRIS

**Population:** Approximately 8 in Australia, very small for the 
tribe globally.
**Primary presence:** Regional WA (around Albany), regional NT 
(around Alice Springs), small presence in regional Tasmania.
**Senior representative:** EINAR HOLMSTAD (Get of Fenris, Athro 
rank, Ahroun auspice, age 56, based in Albany WA; the 
continental Get of Fenris senior representative since 2010).
**Tribal specialty:** Combat-traditionalist tradition, harsh-environment 
specialisation, Wyrm-confrontation as primary operational 
mode.
**Diplomatic register:** Correct-but-cool with Glass Walkers; 
the Get of Fenris consider the Cress Truce ideologically 
suspect (any Garou negotiation with Kindred is, in Get of 
Fenris doctrine, theological compromise) but have not 
violated it across 101 years; respect for Glass Walker 
tribal autonomy prevents direct intervention; will 
reluctantly cooperate with Kindred parties on Wyrm-confrontation 
priorities, will not cooperate on diplomatic-relationship 
priorities.
**Operational note:** Get of Fenris participation in Cress 
Truce proceedings is technical-observer-status (they attend 
but rarely speak); Get of Fenris operations in Australia 
focus on harsh-environment Wyrm-confrontation work in 
remote regional WA, NT, and Tasmania. Einar Holmstad will 
engage with Kindred operatives only in operational 
contexts requiring his specific tribal capabilities; 
will not engage in social or formal-diplomatic contexts.

## UKTENA

**Population:** Approximately 5 in Australia, transplanted from 
Pacific Northwest sept connections.
**Primary presence:** Northern Rivers NSW (around Byron Bay 
and Mullumbimby), small presence in regional Tasmania.
**Senior representative:** YAYA OSHEKI (Uktena, Adren rank, 
Theurge auspice, age 47, based in Byron Bay NSW; arrived 
in Australia in 2008 from a Pacific Northwest Uktena sept 
that lost its caern to Pentex operations).
**Tribal specialty:** Spirit-walker tradition, spirit-mediation 
specialisation, ancestral-knowledge preservation.
**Diplomatic register:** Respectful-and-distant with Glass 
Walkers; the Uktena's Australian presence is recent and 
their primary loyalties remain to their North American 
sept-network; cooperative with Children of Gaia on 
spiritual-mediation matters; cordial with Australian 
Indigenous supernatural traditions through the Uktena's 
North American Indigenous-affinity heritage that produces 
respectful protocol-cooperation register; cooperative 
with Hecata on death-tradition matters; will engage with 
Kindred parties under Cress Truce protocols but rarely 
initiates such engagement.
**Operational note:** Uktena participation in Cress Truce 
proceedings is observer-status; Uktena operations in 
Australia focus on spiritual-knowledge preservation and 
on supporting their displaced North American sept members. 
Yaya Osheki and Mereki Cole have a respectful working 
relationship that handles cross-tribal Theurge cooperation.

## INDIGENOUS AUSTRALIAN SUPERNATURAL TRADITIONS

(Adaptation Note — see Part 1 entry 13)

The canonical werewolf-equivalent presence in Indigenous 
Australian context is NOT modeled in this kit as standard 
Garou. The Glass Walker tribe and adjacent tribes have 
respectful working acknowledgment that Aboriginal Australian 
supernatural traditions have their own protocols distinct 
from the Garou Nation, and cross-tradition diplomatic 
cooperation in Australia operates through informal 
Indigenous-supernatural-elder consultation that the 
Royal Park sept has maintained for several decades.

Tau Larrakia of the Footscray Crew bridges this distinction 
in his unusual position as Indigenous Garou-cooperation-trained 
Kindred. Mereki Cole's dual Wiradjuri-Children-of-Gaia 
heritage produces another bridge.

Chronicles engaging Indigenous Australian supernatural 
traditions more deeply should consult Indigenous-Australian 
sensitivity readers and source material directly rather 
than relying on this kit's intentionally light handling.
