=== COTERIE BINDING ===

The single most important question: what binds them? Coteries break apart 
without binding. Pick one primary binding from below; secondary bindings 
add texture but don't substitute.

[ primary_binding(*pick ONE*):
  □ SHARED CAUSE — political, ideological, or moral commitment all members share
  □ MUTUAL PROTECTION — practical alliance against shared threats
  □ TERRITORIAL — they share haven space or feeding territory
  □ KINSHIP — sired by the same Kindred OR family-of-choice formation
  □ ROMANTIC NETWORK — interlocking romantic/sexual bonds
  □ TRAUMATIC FOUNDATION — survived something together that bonded them
  □ COMMERCIAL — joint operation that requires their specific skills
  □ TRADITIONAL — inherited or formal coterie position
  □ DEBT — mutual obligation that can't be discharged individually
]
